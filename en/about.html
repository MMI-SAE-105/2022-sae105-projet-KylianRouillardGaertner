About

My name is Kylian Rouillard--Gaertner I am a student in MMi in the department Belfort-Montbéliard and I am currently 18 years old. This website is a project realized in the framework of a pedagogical exercise in the MMi department of Montbéliard. My interpretation of the subject "Note for too late" was the following, to create a website that speaks to our future selves while trying to captivate an external audience. The goal was to bring diverse communities together to discover things they might not have imagined they would like.

We can notice this vision with the choice of the presented works. We go from an animated film of love to a dramatic and tragic theater scene. All of this is explained with a deep vision and a language created from a mixture of sustained and current. The aim is to bring together people who are close to literary analysis and those who are not. After all, you don't need to have graduation to understand a rich explanation.

I hope in any case that the site pleases you or will please you. It was made to be the most accessible possible, if you do not find your happiness or that you have difficulty I apologize in advance and you can send me an email to the following address: kylian.gaertner@gmail.com
